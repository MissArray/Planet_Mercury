Mercury

The planet's surface is represented in false colour in the relief-map that was based on data collected by the MESSENGER spacecraft and published in 2016.

A day on Mercury lasts 58.6 hours. In the animation, 1 Mercurian hour = 2 seconds, so one full rotation lasts 117.2 seconds.
